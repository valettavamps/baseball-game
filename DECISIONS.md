## 🎮 FOCUS: What Makes It Fun?

This is the missing piece. Before we build anything, we need to nail:

**What is the core gameplay loop that keeps people coming back?**

### Option 1: Daily Management Sim
- Check in daily
- Set lineups, make trades
- Watch game results
- Adjust strategy
- **Engagement:** HIGH (daily commitment)
- **Fun factor:** Strategy depth, team building
- **Reference:** MLB The Show franchise mode

### Option 2: Passive Investment Game
- Stake at season start
- Check in weekly
- Watch standings & money grow
- Minimal daily actions
- **Engagement:** LOW (set it and forget it)
- **Fun factor:** Financial strategy, picking winners
- **Reference:** Prediction markets, PFL staking

### Option 3: Hybrid (Recommended)
- **Core loop:** Passive staking (low time commitment)
- **Owner layer:** If you own a team, daily management
- **Social layer:** Leaderboards, trash talk, community events
- **Engagement:** FLEXIBLE (casual or hardcore)
- **Fun factor:** Accessible to everyone, depth for those who want it
- **Reference:** Axie Infinity (passive scholars + active managers)
